About Us

Protecting Your African Adventure

We are helping over 1000 adventurers a year frequenting over 300 sportsmen related safari operators, photo safari, holiday tour operators, and a range of guest accommodation providers by acting as the financial intermediary to receive the customers' funds in the USA and Europe then deliver these funds to the vendors in the currency and account of their choosing. We deliver financial services which are prompt, safe, and add layers of certainty to both the adventure seeker and the service provider.

What We Do

Describing our services is simple - we are expressly NOT a travel agent or accommodation provider. Instead, our services should be considered a "consumer protection service" or "concierge service" rather than a tour booking service, hospitality or accommodation provider, or anything other than a financial services intermediary. The bank should acknowledge that our "back of house" website facilities are all dedicated to the movement of payments from customer to service provider which includes the use of the prospective credit card facility (replacing PayPal which is the existing service provider). Easy2Africa helps US customers make certain that the money they pay for an African adventure is delivered to the "right" accommodation provider and based on a legitimate invoice. Our vendor screening process is another layer of security and confidence for the customer.

Why Choose Easy2Africa

Easy2Africa requires all vendors to execute written agreements (not merely "click-thru" contracts) covering service delivery, customer returns, and liability allocations and waivers. While Easy2Africa is "held harmless" by the vendor (and customer) we commit to serve the customer with intervention and consultation actions where required. There is a series of risk mitigation language in the contract the customer agrees to using our "click-thru" platform. The language limits our liability to a maximum of the fees earned by us on the transaction(s); holds us harmless for the conduct of the vendor; immunizes us for acts of g-d, pandemic, government, and terrorism; and provides an affirmative covenant that the customer will/has obtained travel insurance which includes cancellation and interruption
100% coverage. Specific to South Africa, our customer has privacy protections as set forth in the Protection of Personal Information Act (often called the POPI Act or POPIA) which terms and conditions are set forth in the same "click-thru" contract. The US does not have such provisions nationally but we comply with the "opt-put" provisions of the Nevada Privacy of Information Collected on the Internet from Consumers Act (NPICICA) (our State of domicile) by providing a link for such requests. Easy2Africa does not collect personal health information and is therefore free from HIPPA and other similar state laws regarding privacy.
