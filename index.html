About Us

Protecting Your African Adventure

At Easy2Africa, we are dedicated to protecting your African adventure. Unlike travel agents or accommodation providers, we operate as a consumer protection and concierge service, specializing in secure payments and peace of mind for travelers exploring Africa.

What We Do

Our platform is designed to ensure that your payments reach the right service providers, based on legitimate invoices and transparent agreements. We are not a tour booking service or a hospitality provider; instead, we act as a trusted financial services intermediary. Our “back of house” systems focus exclusively on moving your money safely from you to your chosen provider.

Why Choose Easy2Africa

Fraud Protection

We guarantee your payment against fraud and the failure of service providers to deliver as promised. No other payment platform offers this level of seamless customer protection for African tourism.

Verified Providers

If a tour operator is not on the Easy2Africa platform, we advise customers to review them carefully and insist on using our platform for added security.

Refund Assurance

In the rare event that a provider fails to deliver, we are proud to have protected our customers, such as issuing refunds in cases like the Rafiki Safari scam.

Our Commitment

We work closely with both customers and vendors to ensure everyone understands their rights and responsibilities. Our clear, aligned policies set expectations for both sides:

Vendors are required to deliver services exactly as contracted and advertised.

Customers acknowledge, when paying through Easy2Africa, that they have a written agreement with specific terms and conditions from the vendor.

Our mission is simple: to make your African travel experience safer, more transparent, and worry-free. With Easy2Africa, your adventure is protected every step of the way.
